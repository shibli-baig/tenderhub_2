Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about BidSuite and government tender management.

What is BidSuite?

BidSuite is an operational command centre for government tender strategy. It unifies discovery, evaluation, collaboration, and compliance into a single experience built for leadership teams navigating high-value public infrastructure opportunities.

How many government tenders does BidSuite track?

BidSuite indexes over 30,000 government tenders from central, state, and PSU portals, providing comprehensive coverage of public procurement opportunities across India.

What industries does BidSuite support?

BidSuite provides industry vertical blueprints for 19+ sectors including Water Supply, Healthcare, Transport, Urban Development, Energy, Education, and more.

How does AI assist in tender management?

BidSuite uses GPT-powered processors and enterprise-grade vector search to extract structured details from certificates, tender PDFs, and scanned images, enabling AI-driven tender recommendations and automated diligence.

Is BidSuite suitable for enterprise use?

Yes, BidSuite is designed for enterprise use with role-based access control, company-code onboarding, audit-ready logs, encryption, and data residency controls built for enterprise governance.
